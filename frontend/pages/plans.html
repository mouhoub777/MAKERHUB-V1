Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and we adjust billing accordingly.
What's the difference between commission and monthly fees?
Our Freemium plan takes a 10% commission on your revenue. Paid plans have reduced commission (4% for Pro, 2% for Business) plus a fixed monthly subscription. The higher your plan, the lower the commission.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Yes! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to get started.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund you in full.
